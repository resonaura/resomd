## PART 2: VENUE BACKLINE & STAGE HARDWARE REQUIREMENTS

### 1. Audio & Hardware (Provided by Venue)

- **1 x Stage Box** (with at least 11 available inputs, routed to FOH)
- **1 x Bass Combo** (suitable for live performance stage volume)
- **1 x Vocal Microphone Stand** (Boom stand preferred)
- **1 x Guitar Amp Microphone Stand** (Short boom stand)
- **2 x Microphone Cables** (XLR-XLR for mic and vocal processing)
- **2 x Independent Power Outlets** (On stage, near the playback setup and
  vocalist's amp)

### 2. Monitoring (Wedges / IEMs Setup)

- **Stage Monitors / Wedges:** Required on stage for the **Vocalist** and
  **Guitarist**.
  - _Note:_ Vocalist and Guitarist use local, independent IEMs for their
    personal instruments only. Traditional stage wedges are required to monitor
    the rest of the mix (Playback, Bass, Vocals, etc.).
- **Drummer Monitoring:** **Self-provided.** The drummer uses a fully
  self-contained IEM system (including full mix + click/metronome). No venue
  monitor or click routing is required for the drummer.

### 3. Drums

- **Standard Drum Kit Backline**
